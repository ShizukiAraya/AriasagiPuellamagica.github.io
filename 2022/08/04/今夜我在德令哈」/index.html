「姐姐 今夜我在德令哈」

2022/8/4

姐姐，今夜我在德令哈
这是雨水中一座荒凉的城
除了那些路过的和居住的
德令哈……今夜
这是唯一的，最后的，抒情。
这是唯一的，最后的，草原。
我把石头还给石头
让胜利的胜利
今夜青稞只属于她自己
一切都在生长
今夜我只有美丽的戈壁 空空
姐姐，今夜我不关心人类，我只想你

Dear sis, my love
Desperately tonight, exiled to DeLingHa,
murky, and shrouded.
The Erebus left nothing but
Desolated, but the solitary, misanthropic, Gobi desert,
for the pathetic poet.

Dear sis, desperately tonight,
my figure exiled at DeLingHa——
Another town drinking the dismal rain
Except for those left their anima or
their cadaver submerged by ruthless sand
and gravel.

It’s the irreplaceable bursting, finale,
the irrefutable ultimate, finale,
of Moorland born and meant to be barren.
I re-hatched Sisyphus back to his boulder
By-stood Trojans getting sunk in their triumph,
Triumph.

Albeit, those Tibetan barley blooms herself, waywardly,
tonight, those nights.
Every single creature flourishes——
To whom I could find my intimate, mere,
but the ruthlessly Gobi sparkles,
Nothing, Niente, Nihil, Rien.

Sis, beloved
I‘m caring nothing about anthros
Aber peerlessly, obsessively, missing you.
